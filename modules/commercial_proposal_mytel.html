Executive Summary

Add Value System proposes a comprehensive 5-year telecommunications service partnership to provide reliable connectivity between PTTEP's Zawtika platforms (ZPQ-ZWP4). This turnkey solution leverages Mytel's licensed spectrum and regulatory compliance to deliver enterprise-grade services with guaranteed performance.

Key Partnership Value: Mytel becomes the authorized service provider for PTTEP's critical offshore communications, ensuring regulatory compliance and operational excellence through a managed service model.
