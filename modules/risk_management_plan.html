Critical Risks (Score 20-25)

R-001
Probability: 4 Impact: 5 Score: 20

Equipment Delivery Delay

Description: Ceragon manufacturing delays or shipping issues causing project timeline slip beyond PTTEP operational requirements.

Impact: Project delay, continued reliance on unreliable 5GHz system, potential production losses for PTTEP.

Mitigation Strategies:

  • Establish priority manufacturing slot with Ceragon
  • Include penalty clauses in supplier contract
  • Arrange expedited shipping options
  • Maintain weekly communication with Ceragon project manager

Contingency Plan: Source alternative compatible equipment from regional stock if delays exceed 4 weeks.

Owner: Procurement Manager | Review Date: Weekly during procurement phase

R-002
Probability: 4 Impact: 5 Score: 20

Regulatory Approval Delays

Description: Myanmar PTD licensing or import permit delays preventing timely equipment deployment.

Impact: Cannot operate legally, project suspension, financial penalties, reputation damage.

Mitigation Strategies:

  • Leverage Mytel's existing operator license and relationships
  • Submit applications 3 months in advance
  • Engage local regulatory consultant
  • Prepare comprehensive documentation package

Contingency Plan: Escalate through Mytel's government relations team; consider temporary operation under testing license.

Owner: Regulatory Affairs Lead | Review Date: Bi-weekly

High Risks (Score 12-19)

R-003
Probability: 3 Impact: 5 Score: 15

Offshore Installation Weather Delays

Description: Monsoon season or rough seas preventing safe platform access for installation.

Impact: Installation postponement, increased logistics costs, project timeline extension.

Mitigation Strategies:

  • Schedule installation during favorable weather window (December-April)
  • Monitor 14-day weather forecasts
  • Secure backup installation slots
  • Pre-position equipment at shore base

Owner: Installation Manager

R-004
Probability: 3 Impact: 4 Score: 12

Technical Skills Gap

Description: Local team lacks expertise in Ceragon IP-50CX and ATEX installations.

Impact: Installation errors, safety incidents, extended commissioning time, poor system performance.

Mitigation Strategies:

  • Arrange Ceragon certified training for installation team
  • Deploy vendor engineer for critical activities
  • Create detailed installation procedures and checklists
  • Conduct dry-run installation onshore

Owner: Technical Manager

Risk Triggers & Early Warning Signs

Key Risk Indicators to Monitor:

  • Schedule Variance: Any task delayed >5 days triggers risk review
  • Budget Variance: Cost overrun >5% requires escalation
  • Quality Metrics: Failed tests or inspections initiate root cause analysis
  • Vendor Performance: Missed milestones activate contingency plans
  • Regulatory Status: Application processing >30 days triggers escalation
