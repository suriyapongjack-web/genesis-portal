1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Strategic Telecommunications Solution for Offshore Myanmar Operations

Add Value System Company Limited is pleased to present this comprehensive quotation for the deployment of a state-of-the-art telecommunications link connecting PTTEPI's Zawtika offshore platform (ZPQ) to the onshore facility (ZWP4).

This turnkey solution represents a strategic investment in critical infrastructure that will enable PTTEP's digital transformation initiatives while ensuring operational continuity and safety compliance for offshore operations.

Solution Highlights:

  • Proven Ceragon IP-50CX technology with ATEX Zone-2 certification
  • Licensed 7GHz frequency band for interference-free operation
  • Complete turnkey implementation with 5-year comprehensive support
  • 99.95% availability SLA commitment
  • Local presence in Thailand and Myanmar
